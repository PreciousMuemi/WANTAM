Expected answer would cover:

- Life, liberty, security of person
- Freedom of conscience, expression, assembly
- Right to fair trial and due process
- Property rights
- Health, education, social protection

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific**:
   - ✅ "What are workers' rights regarding minimum wage?"
   - ❌ "Tell me about work"

2. **Ask follow-ups**:
   - "Can you explain that more simply?"
   - "What are the exceptions?"
   - "Where can I find more info?"

3. **Ask about recent changes**:
   - "What changed in the Finance Bill?"
   - "What are the new tax laws?"

4. **Ask for practical advice**:
   - "What should I do if my employer doesn't pay minimum wage?"
   - "How do I challenge this in court?"

❌ Represent you in court

You: What is the Constitution?

in 2010. It establishes the structure of government, protects fundamental
rights and freedoms, and serves as the basis for all other laws in the country.

The Constitution created a devolved system of government with both national
and county governments. It also established an independent judiciary and
recognized certain inalienable rights for all Kenyan citizens.

3. Freedom of conscience and religion
4. Freedom of expression and media freedom
5. Freedom of peaceful assembly and association
6. Fair trial rights
7. Protection of property
8. Health, education, and social security
